ABOUT THE ARTIST

Hello! My name is Lovelyn Perez, and I’m an 18 year old artist based in Olongapo City. And this is where I share my art. My current style focuses on experimentation and sketches, allowing me to engage in a variety of media and styles to communicate with my audience. From sketching, inking, watercolor painting, acrylic and colored pencils, markers, I’ve tried it all and can’t wait to see what comes next!
